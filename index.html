About me

UX/UI Reearcher/Designer

My name is Bri & I'm originally from Scranton, PA - now traveling as I work & study remote. I find that working remotely increases my work life balance, being able to do the things I love more often such as arts, reading, & hiking. Always have been passionate about arts & creativity: often painting, producing digital art pieces, learning instruments, or practicing photography. This passion for art led me to begin UX/UI Design. Upon starting UX/UI as someone interested in design, I realized I was much more intrigued by UX Research and fell in love with any & all research processes. The early work & education choices I made in customer service, first aid response, & entertainment have geared me toward truly being able to empathize & understand users in a variety of environments.
